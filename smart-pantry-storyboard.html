Welcome & Value Prop

Onboarding
First‑time user
Business Plan: “Never waste food again”

Goal of this screen

This screen introduces Smart Pantry’s core promise from the business plan: reducing food waste, saving money, and eliminating “what’s for dinner?” stress by using AI and inventory tracking. The user should immediately understand why the app exists and feel motivated to continue.

Key UX decisions

  • Single, bold value statement (“Never waste food again”) aligned with the business concept.
  • Two supporting cards that echo the business plan: inventory visibility and sustainability‑driven savings.
  • Primary CTA: “Get Started” moves into personalization; secondary CTA: “Skip for now” respects busy users.

User story

As a sustainability‑minded millennial with limited time, I want to quickly see how this app will help me waste less food and plan meals, so I can decide if it’s worth setting up right now.
